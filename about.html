Mission & Mandate

Why EPF SSIF Exists

  • Structural unemployment — a multi-generational challenge
  • Conventional CSI fails: capital deployed once, consumed once, gone
  • EPF SSIF closes that loop with a circular, self-sustaining engine
Evergreen Engine

Social spending is structured as a revolving investment pool. Covenant flows and asset revenues are continuously reinvested, creating a compounding multiplier effect on jobs and enterprise output.

Verified, GPS-Tracked Impact

Every asset is geofenced and monitored via the VSI Platform. Job creation, enterprise revenue, and skills training are timestamped, independently audited, and available in real time.

B-BBEE Optimised Returns

A single SSIF commitment simultaneously satisfies ESD, Skills Development, and SED obligations — generating a 3.5x+ macro-multiplier compared to traditional grant-based CSI.

Institutional-Grade Oversight

The Governance Lock

Your capital is protected by multiple layers of institutional safeguards, independent oversight, and asset security protocols designed to eliminate single points of failure.

Separation of Powers
  • Independently managed by Effectus Capital Management (FSCA-regulated)
  • TDM — physical asset custodian
  • Paypoint — all financial transactions
  • VSI Platform — independent data audit
  • The company managing assets never touches the money
Asset-Backed Security & GPS Shield
  • Backed by tangible, recallable physical assets (carts, production kits)
  • GPS geofencing prevents unauthorised asset relocation
  • Physical Asset Shield ensures assets are actively trading and secure

Partner Ecosystem

Built on Specialised Custodians

EPF SSIF separates every function across purpose-built partners — no single entity controls the full stack, eliminating conflicts of interest and single points of failure.

Effectus Capital Management

Fund Manager / FSCA-Regulated Asset Manager

A professional asset management and advisory firm that prioritises precise execution to deliver high performance. They utilise technology and advanced financial engineering to create investment products including hedge funds and funds targeting listed and unlisted innovative companies, while offering advisory services for strategic risk management and financial structuring.

Queensway Capital

Corporate Finance Advisor & Strategic Activation Partner

A growth partner that helps large organisations turn high-level strategies into actual market results. Instead of traditional consulting, they embed experienced fractional leaders within a company to take direct responsibility for outcomes, and use a specialised AI sandbox to test and prove new technologies before full launch — ensuring growth initiatives are data-driven and low-risk.

TDM

Primary Operational & Technical Implementation Partner

TDM is the critical boots-on-the-ground operational vehicle responsible for the physical and technical management of the asset kits (mobile food and cell phone repair carts), overseeing day-to-day deployment, operator onboarding, and supply-chain logistics.

Paypoint

Fintech Infrastructure Partner / “Digital Lock” Enforcer

Paypoint provides the hard-coded technology infrastructure that drives the fund's entire accountability narrative, deploying smart Point-of-Sale (POS) terminals attached to each Business-in-a-Box unit. Separated from asset management and audit.

ENS Africa

Legal Counsel

ENS Africa acts as primary legal counsel responsible for corporate structuring, regulatory compliance, and commercial contracting. They draft all governance documents and ensure that automated cash routing and digital compliance structures align with South African statutory laws.
