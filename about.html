Mission & Mandate

Why EPF SSIF Exists

  • Structural unemployment is a multi-generational challenge
  • Conventional CSI fails: capital deployed once, consumed once, gone
  • EPF SSIF closes that loop with a circular, self-sustaining engine
Evergreen Engine

Social spending is structured as a revolving investment pool. Covenant flows and asset revenues are continuously reinvested, creating a compounding multiplier effect on jobs and enterprise output.

Verified, GPS-Tracked Impact

Every asset is geofenced and monitored via the VSI Platform. Job creation, enterprise revenue, and skills training are timestamped, independently audited, and available in real time.

B-BBEE Optimised Returns

A single Fund 1 commitment simultaneously satisfies Skills Development and SED obligations, generating a 3.5x+ macro-multiplier compared to traditional grant-based CSI. ESD recognition requires an enterprise development loan structure; contact the fund team.

Proof of Concept

135 Mobile Food Carts. Already Deployed. Already Trading.

The EPF SSIF is not asking you to fund a concept. Township Digital Micro-Enterprise (TDM) has already placed 135 mobile food carts across South Africa's township economy. They are deployed, trading, and creating jobs. Right now.

What Fund 1 does is amplify that proven model with your capital, deploying additional GPS-tracked, independently audited assets at scale. Every outcome is reported to you quarterly from Day 1.

The Governance Lock

Your capital is protected by multiple layers of institutional safeguards, independent oversight, and asset security protocols designed to eliminate single points of failure.

Separation of Powers
  • Independently managed by Effectus Capital Management (FSCA-regulated)
  • TDM — physical asset custodian
  • Paypoint — all financial transactions
  • VSI Platform — independent data audit
  • The company managing assets never touches the money
Grant Channel: Zero Repayment Expectation

Your donation is deployed as a grant to verified township entrepreneurs. It is not a loan and carries no repayment expectation. The governance model ensures 100% of deployed capital reaches its intended beneficiaries, tracked, audited, and reported to you quarterly.

Partner Ecosystem

Built on Specialised Custodians

EPF SSIF separates every function across purpose-built partners. No single entity controls the full stack, eliminating conflicts of interest and single points of failure.

Effectus Capital Management

Fund Manager / FSCA-Regulated Asset Manager

Effectus acts as the official, FSCA-regulated financial services provider and asset manager for the ecosystem. While the initial donor channel (Fund 1) focuses on pilot deployments, Effectus steps in to provide the heavy institutional-grade governance, compliance, and fiduciary oversight necessary for Fund 2. They are responsible for managing commercial capital allocations, ensuring strict portfolio risk management, and maintaining investor reporting structures that satisfy traditional asset managers and DFIs.

Queensway Capital

Corporate Finance Advisor & Strategic Activation Partner

Queensway Capital operates as both the structural architect and the execution engine for the fund. On the advisory side, they structure the commercial architecture, manage capital-raising strategies for institutional target tranches, and ensure absolute compliance with institutional investment mandates. As the strategic activation partner, they lead the mobilization of the partner ecosystem, coordinate high-level stakeholder engagement, and oversee the execution frameworks to ensure the operational model successfully scales into a live, high-volume transactional platform.

Township Digital Micro-Enterprise (TDM)

Primary Operational & Technical Implementation Partner

TDM is the critical boots-on-the-ground operational vehicle responsible for the physical and technical management of the asset kits (mobile food and cell phone repair carts), overseeing day-to-day deployment, operator onboarding, and supply-chain logistics.

Paypoint

Fintech Infrastructure Partner / “Digital Lock” Enforcer

Paypoint provides the hard-coded technology infrastructure that drives the fund's entire accountability narrative, deploying smart Point-of-Sale (POS) terminals attached to each Business-in-a-Box unit. Separated from asset management and audit.

ENS Africa

Legal Counsel

ENS Africa acts as primary legal counsel responsible for corporate structuring, regulatory compliance, and commercial contracting. They draft all governance documents and ensure that automated cash routing and digital compliance structures align with South African statutory laws.
