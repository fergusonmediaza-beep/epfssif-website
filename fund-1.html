The SED Problem

Why Most SED Spend Underperforms

SED is the most misunderstood element of the B-BBEE scorecard. Most companies overspend on SED and underdeliver on documentation, leaving points on the table at verification time.

Unverifiable Outcomes

NGO beneficiary counts and community programmes rarely translate into auditable job creation data. Your B-BBEE verification agency cannot accept manual reports.

Receipt Without Proof

You write the cheque and receive a receipt, but the SED pillar requires evidence of socio-economic upliftment, not just a payment confirmation.

Annual Reset

SED spend is consumed annually with no compounding effect. Your budget resets every year, and so does your impact problem.

Audit Exposure

B-BBEE verification agencies increasingly challenge self-reported SED outcomes. Without independent, timestamped data, your points are at risk.

EPF SSIF 1 solves all four: GPS-tracked, Paypoint POS-verified, independently audited SED documentation structured for B-BBEE verification from Day 1.
