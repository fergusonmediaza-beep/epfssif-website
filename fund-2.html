The Instrument

Social Impact Security Instrument (SSIN)

The SSIN is a structured note underpinned by verified, GPS-tracked impact assets deployed in South Africa's township economy. It is designed to give institutional investors — pension funds, development finance institutions, and impact-aligned asset managers — access to quantifiable social returns alongside financial yield.

Unlike traditional impact instruments that rely on self-reported metrics, the SSIN's performance data is independently audited from Day 1 via the VSI Platform — giving institutional investment committees the verified, timestamped evidence they require for ESG reporting, SDG alignment, and regulatory compliance.

Target investor profile: Pension funds, development finance institutions (DFIs), registered impact investment managers, and institutional asset managers with ESG or SDG mandates. Minimum entry threshold and regulatory terms available upon request.
