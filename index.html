The Donation Gap

Why Your Donation Deserves Better

Traditional philanthropy is built on goodwill, but goodwill alone cannot solve a structural unemployment crisis. The challenge is not generosity; it is the mechanism through which generosity flows.

No Proof of Impact

Most donors receive a receipt but no evidence of what their money actually created. How many jobs? How long did they last? Where is the proof?

No Accountability

Manual reporting, cash handling, and unverified milestones leave donors relying on trust. The EPF SSIF replaces trust with technology.

Once-off Value

A traditional donation creates a moment, not a movement. Once spent, the impact stops. The circular engine turns your gift into a perpetual cycle.

No Legacy

Your contribution should build something durable. A township enterprise that creates 10 jobs today will create 20 tomorrow if the capital keeps being reinvested.

EPF SSIF solves all four: with GPS-tracked, timestamped, independently audited impact data structured for B-BBEE verification and Integrated Reporting from Day 1.

Pilot to Platform

A Two-Phase Journey to Scale

Phase 1 donors are not just making a philanthropic contribution; they are funding the institutional proof-of-concept that unlocks R100M+ of commercial capital in Phase 2.

Phase 1 — Active Now (2026)

Build the Proof-of-Concept

Donor and CSI capital deploys the first cohort of entrepreneurs and builds the verified impact data set: 100+ entrepreneurs, 10,000 targeted verified jobs, fully audited. Early Phase 1 contributors are the anchor proof-of-concept.

Phase 2 — 2027

Unlock Institutional Capital

Once Phase 1 proof points are established, institutional capital enters via the Social Impact Security Instrument (SSIN), targeting pension funds, DFIs, and impact investors. Your audited Phase 1 outcomes are the cornerstone of the Phase 2 institutional offer.
